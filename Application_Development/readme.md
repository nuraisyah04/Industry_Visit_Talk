I recently participated in our Industrial Day for the Application Development course, and it was a great experience! We met with our client, Dr Norhanisah from Tadika Aqil Mikail (Genius Aulad), to dive deep into their needs for a new Teacher Management System.

Right now, we are in the early stages of the project. We’ve gathered all the requirements and are now tasked with developing an application that covers 6 core modules. The goal is to build a digital solution that helps the kindergarten manage their teachers and daily operations more efficiently, moving away from manual paperwork.

Looking forward to the development process and can’t wait to see how our 6 modules come together to help the teachers at Tadika Aqil Mikail. Stay tuned for updates! 🚀
